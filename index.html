📥 HOW TO EXPORT FROM TRADINGVIEW

1
Open TradingView and go to your account. Click your profile icon top right → My Profile
2
Click the Trading tab → then Trade History or open your Paper Trading / Live Broker account panel at the bottom of the chart
3
In the bottom panel, click Trade History tab → look for the Export / Download icon (looks like ↓ or a cloud icon) → click it
4
Save the .csv file to your Downloads folder
5
Come back here → drag & drop the CSV file onto the import zone above — or click Browse to find it
Supported formats
✅ TradingView Paper Trading export
✅ TradingView Broker integration export
✅ Generic CSV with: Date, Symbol, Side, Qty, Price, P&L columns
✅ Any broker CSV that includes trade open/close data
